## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the minimum commitment?**  
A: Our Premium tier operates on a month-to-month basis with 30-day cancellation notice. Platinum and Elite tiers require quarterly minimum commitments for planning stability.

**Q: Do you manage multiple properties?**  
A: Yes. Platinum and Elite clients benefit from multi-property oversight. We coordinate across locations seamlessly.

**Q: How do you handle emergencies?**  
A: We maintain 24/7 emergency protocols. Critical situations (water damage, security breaches, power loss) trigger immediate response from our network. You're notified in real-time.

**Q: Can I customize my service package?**  
A: Absolutely. Premium clients can add à la carte services. Platinum and Elite clients have full customization options.

**Q: How are vendors selected and vetted?**  
A: All vendors undergo rigorous vetting: background checks, reference verification, insurance validation, and performance evaluations. We maintain relationships with only the most trusted professionals.

**Q: What about privacy?**  
A: Privacy is foundational to our model. All staff sign NDAs. We employ sophisticated security protocols and compartmentalize information strictly.

**Q: Can you help with smart home technology?**  
A: Yes. We advise on and integrate smart home systems compatible with your existing infrastructure. Our team provides training and ongoing support.

**Q: How do you communicate updates?**  
A: Based on your preferences: weekly reports, real-time alerts for critical issues, monthly summaries, or custom communication schedules.
